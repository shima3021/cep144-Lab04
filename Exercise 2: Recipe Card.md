# "Pesto Pasta"
---
## Ingridients:

- Your favorite pasta
- Prepared basil pesto
- Cheese
  - Parmesan is prefered
    
    ---
### Cooking Steps:

1. Bering water to boil and boil your pasta
2. Drain your pasta, and save 1/4 cup of pasta water
3. Toss the pasta with a jar of prepared basil pesto
4. Add pasta water to them to make a silky sauce
5. Top with cheese

**Important cooking tips: you can choose any cheese you like, parmesan is more common. you can add any kinds of veggies or proteins based on your interests. check for your pasta cooking time, but usually 7 to 10 mintues is enough.**
